CRT Technology

Casing Drive System™ & Compact Casing Drive System™

Our Casing Running Tool (CRT) technology — the Casing Drive System™ and Compact Casing Drive System™ — enables casing to be simultaneously gripped, rotated, reciprocated, and circulated directly from the top drive.

This top-drive-mounted approach removes personnel from the red zone, improves connection integrity, and increases the speed and safety of running casing into the well.
