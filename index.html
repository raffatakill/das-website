Our Story

A trusted name in the Middle East since 1994.

Dar Al Sahra Energy Services, owned by Mr. Mohammed Saif Al Khamisi, has been part of the Middle East oil & gas industry for three decades.

DAS is recognized as the first “Local Company” to achieve API Spec Q2 Certification for Quality Management Registration, alongside ISO 9001:2015 — a standard we hold ourselves to on every well, every day.
